These are the current players!

As of the 2023-2024 season, Real Madrid's squad is a blend of experienced veterans and promising young talents, reflecting the club's commitment to maintaining a competitive edge while planning for the future. The defensive line is anchored by stalwarts like Dani Carvajal and David Alaba, offering both experience and tactical acumen. Goalkeeper Thibaut Courtois continues to be a reliable presence between the posts, known for his shot-stopping abilities and command of the penalty area. In midfield, the addition of Jude Bellingham has injected new life and versatility, joining seasoned players such as Luka Modrić and Toni Kroos, who provide creativity and stability.

In the attack, the departure of Karim Benzema marked the end of an era, prompting a tactical shift within the team. Jude Bellingham has taken on a more advanced role, often fulfilling the position left by Benzema. Bellingham's versatility allows him to operate as an attacking midfielder or even a false nine, contributing to the team's offensive play with his creativity, goal-scoring, and ability to link up with other forwards like Vinícius Júnior and Rodrygo. This strategic adaptation has helped maintain Real Madrid's attacking prowess, ensuring the club remains a formidable force in both domestic and European competitions.
